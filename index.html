My Favorite Architecture

Pantheon in Rome

I love the Pantheon in Rome Italy. This building is magnificent because of the breakthrough science that went into it. The building shows the perfection of mathematics, the inside fits a perfect spear and there is a pattern of circles and squares throughout, even the ceiling! The building is meant to be looked at from the inside where you can see the oculus which many believe it represents the seeing eye of God.

Flavian Amphitheater (The Colosseum)

The Colosseum looks beautiful, and I love the detail of the columns. The top column is Greek Corinthian then Ionic then Doric. The colosseum was not only beautiful but it was used for entertainment. Entertainment included Animal battles. Gladiator battles, ship battles, theater shows, and public execution. This is one of my favorites because Romans made their architecture magnificent and useful.
