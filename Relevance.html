The importance of art in my major

Relevance

In the information systems major we deal a lot with data analytics, programming, and development. My favorite part of that is the art of making websites. Websites are technical but also artistic, we use different elements of art so that the websites are presentable and user-friendly. I decided to make a website displaying my favorite Roman architecture to display information and facts about the different pieces. Art is relevant in my major because it helps us understand different forms, after all the Romans had incredible math and engineering. Information systems help art advance as well, it provides the user with information on art pieces and educated the public. It helps tourist plan trips to go see these amazing pieces of architecture. It is also important to know of these great civilizations and the contribution they made to our world. Just the other day I had to make a program that converted decimals to Roman Numerals and vice versa. This information helps artists, such as architects advance in their creations. It is because of the advancement of technology that allows us to discover the truth of art or helps us advance and preserve art. I know I will use this information in my life and in my major and it has made me a well-rounded person.
